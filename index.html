JUICY GRILLS

Chicken Barbecue

8 chicken drumsticks (chicken laps) 1 small bowl barbecue sauce 1 big stock cube (Maggi, Knorr etc) 1 teaspoon thyme Salt (to taste) Black pepper powder (to taste) Onion powder (to taste)

Shawarma

Chicken breasts (de-boned or boneless) Chicken thighs/laps (de-boned or boneless) Shawarma bread (pita bread or tortilla wraps) Small cabbage Medium sized carrots (grated or cut into thin strips) 1 large cucumber (cut into thin strips and circles) 2 big tomatoes (cut into thin strips) 1 medium sized onion (cut into thin strips) Ketchup Mayonnaise Vegetable or olive oil (for frying)

OrangeBerry Smoothie

1 cup of orange juice, squeezed from oranges. 3/4 cups of strawberries, cut into small chunks 3/4 cups of blueberries, cut into small chunks 3 tbsps. of Dano powdered milk dissolved in water to make 1 cup of milk 1 cup of ice 1 tsp. of sugar
